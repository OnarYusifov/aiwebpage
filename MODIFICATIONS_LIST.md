4. **Updated Navigation Menu**: Added "Contact" link to navigation menu on all pages (Home, About, Blogs, Contact)

5. **Changed Color Scheme**: Replaced green color scheme with purple-to-teal gradient theme (primary: #805AD5, secondary: #38B2AC)

6. **Updated Footer**: Added social media links (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram) to footer on all pages, updated footer content and links

7. **Changed Font**: Replaced Manrope font with JetBrains Mono from Google Fonts throughout the entire website

8. **Fixed Hero Section Colors**: Ensured hero section text is readable with white text (#FFFFFF) on dark background (#1A202C) with text shadows for better visibility

9. **Updated Content**: Changed generic template content to business-focused content (e.g., "Modern Solutions for Your Business Needs", updated service descriptions)

10. **Fixed Text Colors**: Updated text colors in light sections (Services section, etc.) to use dark text (#2D3748) on light background (#F7FAFC)

11. **Updated Favicons**: Changed all favicon references from default names to "byart-*" naming convention

12. **Enhanced Buttons**: Updated buttons to use gradient backgrounds (purple to teal) with hover effects and shadows

13. **Improved Styling**: Added custom CSS animations, transitions, hover effects, and enhanced visual styling throughout

14. **Updated Services Section**: Changed service titles and descriptions to be more specific (Web Development, UI/UX Design, Digital Strategy)

15. **Fixed Logo Colors**: Made logo white in navigation (dark background) and dark in footer (light background)
